our story

Three friends, one crazy idea.

In 2019, Alex, Priya, and Marcus were sitting in a cramped apartment arguing about bad design—and realized no one was doing it better. So they quit their jobs on the same day, pooled their savings, and started BETA.

Five years later, we've grown from three laptops on a kitchen table to a 40-person studio trusted by startups and Fortune 500s alike. The friendship is still the foundation of everything.
